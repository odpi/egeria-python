## Mission
>	**Input Required**: False

>	**Description**: The project mission.

The mission is to integrate and normalize sales forecasts across all regions that is understood throughout the company. Sales forecasts need to use a consistent methodologies, and roll up across regions and product lines. They need to be translated into the local languages.

## Purposes
>	**Input Required**: False

>	**Description**: A list of  project purposes.

Normalizing Sales Forecasting is a key step in our transition to a multinational business. It establishes a shared understanding of each region and product line that supports both local and cross-organizational actions. It provides visibility into what is going well and where the different teams need to come together to help each other and the business as a whole.
